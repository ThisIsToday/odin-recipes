Jambalaya

Description

This is a great recipe for jambalya. It is a family secret recipe now now available to the public. Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Andouille sausage
  • Bacon
  • Chicken stock
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Red and green peppers
  • Hot sauce

Directions

  1. Saute the bacon and aromatics. You want to chop the veggies finely.
  2. Add the cajun sausage and cook that under it begins to crisp up a bit. The bacon, sausage in addition to the aromatics we cooked in the first step are going to be the flavor for this delicious and easy jambalaya.
  3. Now add the rice, stock and hot sauce. You just cook this like you would a pot of rice. So simple!
