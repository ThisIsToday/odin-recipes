Boiled Eggs

Description

This is a recipe for perfect boiled eggs. No more peeling piece by piece by piece of egg shells.

Ingredients

  • 1 dozen eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Directions

  1. Put eggs in large pott with lid
  2. Pour cold water over eggs untill fully submerged and add baking soda.
  3. Bring water to a boil then turn off the heat and cover the pot with the lid
  4. 3 minutes for soft boiled. 6 for medium boiled. 12 for hard boiled.
  5. Place in bowl of ice water and allow to cool completely before peeling.
