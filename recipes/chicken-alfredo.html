Chicken Alfredo

Description

Trying to figure out what to have for dinner tonight? Why not try this scruptous chicken alfredo dish. This recipe is probably not the first one you came acrossed while in your search for something to eat tonight but it's the one you are on right now. I can bet your bottm dollar that this is the one you will settle on. The basil, ginger, alfredo sauec is to die for. It will leave you wanting more. The chicken is lathered in chicken seasoning and grilled at 400 degrees for 25 minutes till tender, yet juicy.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 c. whole milk
  • 8 oz. fettuccine
  • 2 c. low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 c. heavy cream
  • 1 c. freshly grated Parmesan

Directions

  1. In large skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil. Add chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook until golden and cooked through, 8 minutes per side. Let rest 10 minutes, then slice.
  2. Add milk, broth, and garlic to skillet. Season with salt and pepper and bring to a simmer. Add fettuccine, stirring frequently for about 3 minutes. Let cook until al dente, 8 minutes more.
  3. Stir in heavy cream and Parmesan until combined. Simmer until sauce thickens.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in sliced chicken. Garnish with parsley.
